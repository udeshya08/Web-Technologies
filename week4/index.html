FIFA 23

FIFA 23 is a football simulation video game released world wide on 30 september 2022 for multiple platforms. It is published by Electronics Arts EA and it is the 30th installment in the FIFA series develop by EA sports. Mbappe and Sam Kerr are the cover footballers for the game.
